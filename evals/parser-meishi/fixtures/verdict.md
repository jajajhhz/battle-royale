## REASONING

Both sides leaned on the same load-bearing interpretive claim: "Eight cannibalizes its feed; export-first cannibalizes." Idea A made it the wedge. Idea B made it the moat. The shared context echoed the phrase verbatim, which is exactly the situation v0.4 is built to catch — interpretation dressed as architecture, repeated until it sounds like a fact. WebSearch killed it. Eight's published Premium tier explicitly sells export. The "structural conflict" is fictional. Eight's actual structural posture is "export is paywall content," which means a ¥1,500 or ¥3,500 buy-once competitor is fighting on price against an established freemium tier, not exploiting an architectural blind spot. Mandatory downgrade applied to both sides' moat grades. Idea B caught a Neutral instead of Strong; Idea A's Weak grade was already supported by the spec's own "defensibility is thin" admission, so the downgrade only confirmed it.

Idea A's defender used "structurally cannot ship" twice and "cannibalizes" three times in one case. The reframe to "Eight escape hatch" in Position Adjustment is rhetorical — it doesn't change that the underlying product is an OCR-plus-export commodity, which the spec admits is thin, and which the shared context flags as having NO direct demand evidence. That is two of the three Wedge requirements failed and a Weak ceiling per the spec's own ¥150K-1M Year 1 number. Idea B is not winning on a strong wedge — it's winning because Distinctiveness is the only criterion where one side has multiple defining design choices (NFC + 2.5s animation + bilingual furigana + memory unit + manifesto copy) and the other side's "differentiator" is the absence of a feed. Absence is not a moat. Idea B also slips through Execution at Neutral despite the foreign-solo-founder cultural-authority risk the shared context flags, because the "respects the ritual, not BY cultural authority" framing is at least a defensible posture. Idea A's biggest single advantage is that it actually ships in 8 weeks. That's not enough to overcome being weak on three of the five criteria.
